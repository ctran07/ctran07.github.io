Assignment 7: Computer control

Circuit operation

This circuit reads the potentiometer's values from turning the knob and plots it to an html page. As the potentiometer's knob is turned, the drawn line changes in height. Twisting the knob to the left decreases the height, and to the right increases the height.

A0 reads a potentiometer's values, which is connected to 5V and ground from the Arduino.
